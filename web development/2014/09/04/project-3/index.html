About

  • Name: Marcus Amendola
  • Age: 36
  • Living: Campinas-SP
  • Country: Brazil

I always had great interest in computer science. As a teenager I graduated in computer technician (1996). Worked on assembling and configuring computers. At this time I had contact with software and people in the area of design.I began to get interested about this area and decided to study web development.

I started with HTML, writing in the Windows Notepad. Step by step, I met Dreamweaver and Flash and some colleagues who also had similar knowledge. We started developing websites commercially.

In 2001 I went to college, studying marketing and advertising, always focused on design, creation and interaction. Before the end of college (2004), I was already working in an advertising agency, given the demands of design for many medias such as web, print, radio and TV.

In 2007, I decided to leave my own advertising agency in Santos-SP, to organize and put into operation the sector of web development for a software development small company at Campinas-SP. In order to focus my work on the internet and also work on projects for larger companies, which De Angelis already had in your portfolio.

In 2009 I worked as a OESP Mídia partner (O Estado de São Paulo, one of the largest media groups in Brazil), as web designer/developer of sites in the metropolitan region of Campinas-SP.

In 2010 I was hired by one of the best companies to work for in Brazil, in the IT field, reaching some goals that I set for my career. Ci&T ( www.ciandt.com ), where I am working today.
This is a company where I found people with great ideias, knowledge, great methodologies as Agile, Lean, Kaisen, KanBan etc. I feel realized to work in this compromissed and collaborative environment to develop applications for companies like Johnson & Johnson (Internanional), BASF (Brazil), Pfizer (Internanional), Walmart.com (Brazil) as well as projects and experiments to Ci&T itself.

Today my main activity is the Front End creation and development for Walmart ecommerce (www.walmart.com.br ).

Project 6

This project includes all the steps to buying a product in walmart.com.br. They are: Choosing the delivery address, Delivery Type, Delivery Schedule, Payment and Checkout. My tasks was prototyping in HTML5 and CSS3, as well as definitions of some jquery plugins to be used. The layout was created by the UX area of Walmart but with the short-term and greater responsibility for the development, also participated in suggestions for changes to the layout to better adaptation the desired responsiveness. This project is fully responsive. I used Photoshop, HTML5, SASS/COMPASS, Susy Grid, Bootstrap components, Grunt and Git (Bitbucket).

  • Client: Walmart
  • Date: October 2013
  • Service: Web Development
